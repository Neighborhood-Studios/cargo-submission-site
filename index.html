The Pilot

How It Works

This pilot is about deal judgment. Show us how you spot, validate, and explain a flip opportunity. No vehicles will be purchased during this test.

01

Find a Candidate

Search any listing platform. Submit vehicles priced under $50,000 with a believable path to resale profit.

02

Evaluate the Deal

Look for roughly $3,000+ gross profit after detail and inspection. Don't assume repairs. Flag anything we'd need to verify.

03

Build Your Case

Explain the buy price, resale price, demand, comps, timeline, and risks. Your reasoning matters as much as the listing.

04

Submit and Get Paid

Submit up to 3 opportunities during the pilot. We'll review each one and process payment after review.

What to Include

  • Listing link
  • Year, make, model, trim
  • Mileage, price, and location
  • Your deal thesis
  • Expected resale price
  • Supporting comps or data
  • Why it can sell within 30 days
  • Risks or red flags

What We're Looking For

  • Listed under $50,000
  • Clear path to $3,000+ gross profit (buy at $25K, sell at $28K+)
  • Likely resale within 30 days
  • No major repairs assumed
  • Strong comps or demand signal
  • Any make, model, or category
  • No project cars
  • No vague "maybe it works" flips
